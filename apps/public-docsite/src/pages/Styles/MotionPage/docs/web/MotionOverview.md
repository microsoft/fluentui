Fluent motion serves a purpose in your app. It gives intelligent feedback based on the user's behavior, keeps the UI feeling alive, and guides the user's navigation through your app. Fluent motion elicits an emotional connection between a user and their digital experience. We build on a foundation of natural movement the user already understands from the physical world, and we extend our system from there. [Learn more about Fluent motion principles](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/uwp/design/motion/).
